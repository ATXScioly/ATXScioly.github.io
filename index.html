About Us

The UT Science Olympiad Alumni Association is an organization of undergraduate and graduate students, as well as faculty members at the University of Texas at Austin, dedicated to spreading Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) education throughout middle and high schools in central Texas through Science Olympiad, a nationally recognized academic competition. The organizationâ€™s goals are to foster enthusiasm and interest in STEM, as well as promoting teamwork, sportsmanship, and the spirit of competition, by establishing new Science Olympiad teams in schools where such extracurriculars are not currently present, mentoring students in STEM subjects outside of the classroom curriculum, and providing opportunities for competition.

2018 Fall Invitational

Tournament Update

Tournament headquarters for check in, information, etc will be in Mezes Hall. Homerooms will now be split between Parlin Hall (PAR), Mezes Hall (MEZ), and McCombs School of Business (CBA). Team check-in will begin at 6:30 AM at Mezes Hall. The best drop off location in the morning will most likely be on 21st Street in front of Littlefield Fountain, which is also marked on the map.

Parking closest to homerooms will be at Brazos garage, and more parking info can be found here. If you requested a bus permit, those will be emailed to you in a follow-up email. Bus parking information can be found in the permits.

The awards ceremony will be at 6:30 PM at UTC 2.112A. UTC is across 21st street from CBA.

As many of you might have heard, Austin has issued a city-wide mandatory boil water notice. This means that water fountains will not be available on the UT Campus during the tournament. The city has stated that water use is safe for activities such as bathing and laundry, but any water intended for consumption should be boiled. With this in mind, we urge you to bring water supplies for your team. Vending machines and stores will still carry bottled water as well. Many restaurants on Guadalupe Street, which borders the UT Campus, might be closed as well. For more information on the boil water notice, please visit austintexas.gov. Your and your students' safety is of paramount importance to us.

Details

The 2018 UT Austin Invitational tournament will be held on October 27, 2018 at the University of Texas at Austin.

The tournament will feature tests written by faculty as well as experienced former competitors. All 23 national events and the 5 state events will be run. If you have any questions, please send them to atxscioly+tournament@gmail.com. Note: registration is closed for the invitational at this time!
